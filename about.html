Who We Are

Welcome to MACE Evangelical Union, a fellowship committed to fostering spiritual growth and vibrant community among college students.

Our Story

The MACE Evangelical Union (EU) is a Christ-centered student fellowship dedicated to nurturing college students in their faith journey while enriching their campus experience. Born from a vision to see students transformed by Christ, we have been a vibrant spiritual home on campus for many years. We are a community where students can connect, grow, and serve, building a foundation of faith that lasts a lifetime.

From humble beginnings, MACE EU has grown through dedicated prayer, faithful Bible study, and genuine fellowship. We believe in creating a welcoming environment where every student feels valued and can explore their faith freely. Our journey is one of continuous growth, guided by God's Word and the Holy Spirit.

Our Aims

Evangelism

UESI aims to share the message of Jesus Christ with post-matric students in India, leading them to a personal experience of salvation through faith in Him.

Discipleship

UESI focuses on nurturing students, helping them grow in their faith and become followers of Jesus Christ. This involves providing resources, support, and opportunities for spiritual growth.

Christian Testimony

UESI encourages students to live out their faith authentically and to be a positive influence on their campuses, demonstrating Christian values and principles.

God's Mission

UESI helps students understand God's global mission and how they can participate in it, whether through local church involvement or through global missions.

Our Affiliation

MACE EU is proudly affiliated with the Union of Evangelical Students of India (UESI). UESI is a nationwide movement dedicated to evangelizing post-matric students across India and nurturing them as devoted disciples of Jesus Christ. This affiliation connects us to a larger network of student fellowships, providing resources, training, and a broader vision for impacting the nation for Christ.

Being part of UESI strengthens our mission, allowing us to participate in national events, leadership development programs, and inter-collegiate initiatives, all aimed at building God's Kingdom through students.
