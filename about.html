Our Fellowship

The MACE Evangelical Union (EU) is a Christ-centered student fellowship dedicated to nurturing college students in their faith journey while enriching their campus experience. We are a vibrant community where students can connect, grow, and serve.

We provide a welcoming environment for worship, in-depth Bible study, fostering meaningful friendships, and mutual support through the unique challenges of college life. Our aim is to equip students to live out their faith authentically in all areas of their lives.

MACE EU is proudly affiliated with the **Union of Evangelical Students of India (UESI)**, a nationwide movement committed to evangelizing students across India and nurturing them as devoted disciples of Jesus Christ. Through this affiliation, we are part of a larger network impacting the nation for Christ.
