Our Vision

Transformed students impacting the campuses and the nation as disciples of The Lord Jesus Christ.

Our Mission

UESI seeks to evangelize post-matric students in India, nurture them as disciples of the Lord Jesus Christ, that they may serve the Church and the society.

The MACE Evangelical Union (EU) is a dynamic Christian fellowship for college students who desire to grow in their relationship with Jesus Christ while navigating campus life. We provide a welcoming community where students can worship together, study God's Word, develop meaningful friendships, and support one another through the challenges of college life.
