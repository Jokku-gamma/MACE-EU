Our Activities

At MACE EU, our vibrant community thrives through a variety of engaging and enriching activities designed for spiritual growth, fellowship, and outreach. Discover how you can connect and participate!

Explore Our Activities

SGMM (Student-Graduate Monthly Meetup)

Students and graduates meet up once every month for praying for our campus, planning new events, sharing testimonies, and fostering mentorship relationships. It's a vital time for connection and strategic prayer.

Home Camp

Students stay together in a graduate's house as their own home. During these immersive camps, we deepen our faith through intensive prayer for the campus, enjoy fellowship, cook meals together, and share life-transforming experiences.

Weekly Bible Studies

Engage in deep discussions and grow in faith through our regular Bible study sessions, held both on campus and online. We delve into scripture, share insights, and apply biblical principles to our daily lives.

Prayer Meetings

Come together to pray for our campus, personal needs, and global concerns in a supportive and uplifting environment. These gatherings strengthen our communal bond and deepen our reliance on God.

Fellowship Gatherings

Build meaningful relationships through our regular fellowship events, outings, and small group discussions. We share joy, support each other, and grow together in a loving community.

Mini Prayer Cell (MPC)

Each student is connected with a graduate for sharing their worries, prayer requests, and life updates in a confidential and supportive setting. MPCs provide personalized mentorship and accountability.

Prayer Walk

Students walk through the campus, praying specifically for the campus environment, the faculty, the staff, and their fellow students. It's a powerful way to intercede for our academic community.
